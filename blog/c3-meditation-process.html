Meditation Process

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

I really struggled to resist the urge to change things from my wireframes mid way through coding it into the HTML and styling it, either to make it easier because I couldn't figure out how to make it do what I was wanting it to do, or because I thought it would make it look better.

I kept the 'process over product' mantra in my mind and resisted the urge to not follow my wireframes. I am glad in the end though because there is a little bit of satisfaction with being able to get from something I have drawn with a pen, to what is a functional webpage.

Search Inside Yourself, Chapter 2: Breathing as if you're life depends on it

I found the 2 minutes quite relaxing. The book talks a lot about how the mind's focus can be trained like a muscle. The instruction is to focus on your breathing, and when your mind starts to wander, gently bring it back to focus. I can feel this happening over and over again, and it does feel like I'm working out my brain. This focus training also reminds you of the wonders that go on inside your mind and that it is capable of a lot of amazing things.
