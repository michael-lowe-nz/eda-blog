Daily Mindfulness

What Did I Learn from my Mindfulness Conversation?

I have been thinking a bit about mindful converstaions. I haven't engaged in a formal conversation, but I am doing some mindful listening to people in my everyday life. I work at a cafe, so it is really essential that I can listen properly and take down people's orders.

I think it's useful to be mindful in this context, beause getting the order right the first time is essential in maintaining the work flow of the coffee, food and till. I am trying to be mindful in listening to the customers, and just by trying to be mindful, I find I can make people's experience slightly better.

My Everyday Mindfulness

As outlined in chapter 3 of Search Inside Yourself, I've been doing a few mindful activities. I've been doing some mindful walking, when I'm just walking somewhere by myself, I like to think about each step connecting my foot to the ground, how my lungs are feeling etc.

At the gym as well, I've sometimes been doing a bit of mindful warmup. I'll jog for about 8 minutes on the treadmill, and really listen to how my body is feeling, how my heart starts pumping faster, and my breathing starts to get heavier. All of this mindfulness is slowly becoming more prominent, but I still feel like I have a way to go before I get any sort of tangible benefit.
