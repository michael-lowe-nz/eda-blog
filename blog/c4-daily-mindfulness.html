Daily Mindfulness

What Did I Learn from my Mindfulness Conversation?

I have been thinking a bit about mindful converstaions. I haven't engaged in a formal conversation, but I am doing some mindful listening to people in my everyday life. I work at a cafe, so it is really essential that I can listen properly and take down people's orders.

I think it's useful to be mindful in this context, beause getting the order right the first time is essential in maintaining the work flow of the coffee, food and till. I am trying to be mindful in listening to the customers, and just by trying to be mindful, I find I can make people's experience slightly better.
